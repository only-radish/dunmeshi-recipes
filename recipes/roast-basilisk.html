Roast Basilisk

ローストバジリスク

Description

A full bird of roast basilisk, stuffed to the brim with healing herbs to combat against the petrifying bite of the basilisk itself. A perfect source of both protein and fibre, this dish will fill up any adventurer for the journey ahead.

Ingredients

serves 6

  • 1 Basilisk -- bird (remove snake tail)
  • Mana herbs to taste
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Stuffing:
    • 1 medium antidotal herb
    • 1 bunch of strong medicinal herbs
    • 1 bunch of ultra medicinal herbs
    • 1 medium anti-paralysis herb
    • 1 medium anti-petrification herb

Steps

  1. Prepare the basilisk meat.

    2. Cut off the snake tail and legs of the basilisk. Scald the body briefly in hot water and pluck out the feathers. Remove the innards. Rub in spices and mana herbs. Rest overnight if possible for best results.

  2. Prepare the herb stuffing.

    3. Wash and dice up herbs to size. Stuff the basilisk meat with the diced herbs and tie the opening shut.

  3. Roast the basilisk.

    4. Skewer the stuffed bird onto a large skewer and roast slowly on an open fire. Once golden brown and thoroughly cooked, enjoy!
